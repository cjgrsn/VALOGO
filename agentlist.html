Valorant Agents

Learn more about the agents and their unique abilities.

Agents Role Descriptions
Sage Sentinel A healer who restores health, creates barriers, and slows enemies. Essential for survival.
Jett Duelist An agile duelist with dashes and vertical movement. Deadly with her throwing knives.
Omen Controller A shadowy figure who blinds, teleports, and deploys smokes to control vision.
Cypher Sentinel A surveillance expert who sets traps and cameras to reveal enemies.
Viper Controller A toxic specialist who deploys poison clouds and walls to damage enemies.
Phoenix Duelist Controls fire walls, flash bursts, and has a self-reviving ultimate.
Reyna Duelist Thrives on kills, healing herself or becoming intangible with orbs.
Killjoy Sentinel A tech genius who deploys turrets and nanoswarms to control areas.
Brimstone Controller Provides smokes, fire grenades, and an orbital strike ultimate.
Skye Initiator A support agent who heals allies and sends seekers to track enemies.
Chamber Sentinel A stylish marksman with traps and a powerful custom sniper ultimate.
Neon Duelist A fast duelist who sprints, slides, and uses electric powers to overwhelm foes.
